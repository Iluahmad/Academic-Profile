About Me

I am Ahmad Ibraheem Ilu born and raised in Kano, Nigeria. I had all my previous education here in Nigeria, Undergraduate training at Northwest University Kano, and Postgraduate Studies to obtain M.Sc in Economics at Bayero University, Kano. I am currently an Economist/ Policy Researcher at the Nigerian Economic Summit Group (NESG), the First and Foremost Think-Tank in Nigeria. Alongside my routine engagements at the NESG, I am part of the NESG Five Men delegation sitting at the Ongoing Presidential Fiscal Policy and Tax Reforms Committee (PFPTR), a committee inaugurated in August 2023 by the President Federal Republic of Nigeria to oversee systemic Fiscal Policy and Tax reforms in Nigeria. I was previously an Assistant Lecturer at the Department of Economics at the Federal University Dutsin-Ma, Katsina State, Nigeria.
